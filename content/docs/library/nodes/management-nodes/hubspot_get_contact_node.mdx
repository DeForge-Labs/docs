## Description
The HubSpot - Get Contact node allows you to retrieve detailed information about a contact stored in your HubSpot CRM. You can search for a contact using their email address or unique HubSpot ID. This node is essential for workflows that require access to contact data for personalization, follow-ups, or analytics.

### Common Use Cases
- Fetching contact details for personalized email campaigns.
- Retrieving contact information for customer support interactions.
- Accessing contact data for sales follow-ups and lead nurturing.
