## Description
The Notion List Pages node allows you to search for specific pages in your Notion workspace and retrieve their IDs and other metadata. This is useful for finding the right pages to work with in your workflows, especially in larger workspaces with many pages. You can provide an optional query to filter the pages by title, making it easier to locate specific pages based on keywords or naming conventions.

### Common Use Cases
- Search for pages in Notion to retrieve their IDs for use in other nodes.
- Filter pages by title to find the right one for your workflow.
- Use the page IDs in other Notion nodes to update content, manage pages, or query data.
